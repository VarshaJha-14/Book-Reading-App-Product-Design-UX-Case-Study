# 📚 BOOKUEY
### Reading Companion App | UX & Product Design Case Study

> A mobile-first reading companion designed to help readers discover books, stay engaged, track reading progress, and create a distraction-free reading experience.

---

## 📌 Overview

BOOKUEY is a conceptual mobile application that reimagines the digital reading experience by combining book discovery, reading progress tracking, note-taking, and personalization into a single platform.

The project focuses on creating a seamless experience that encourages users to build consistent reading habits while reducing the friction of switching between multiple applications for reading, annotations, and organization.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Most reading applications focus primarily on displaying books but offer limited support for maintaining long-term engagement.

Readers often struggle with:

- Losing track of reading progress
- Managing notes separately
- Discovering books aligned with their interests
- Staying focused during reading sessions
- Personalizing the reading experience

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

BOOKUEY combines reading, organization, and productivity into one experience by enabling users to:

- Discover new books
- Track reading progress
- Continue reading seamlessly
- Save books for later
- Highlight important passages
- Create personal notes
- Bookmark pages
- Customize reading themes
